  • Small Virtual Reality Memory Game for ENSF 545 - Virtual Reality.
    • Two containers sit at the bottom of a slide. One to the left of the slide and one to the right.
    • Before anything happens, numbers from 1 to 10 are shown above each container.
    • After a short time the numbers go away and small boxes start coming down the slide.
    • Each of these boxes sliding down the ramp has a number on it.
    • The objective of the game is then to grab the boxes with a haptic controller as they slide down the ramp, and to put them in the correct box based on the number
    • 3D Glasses were used to give the player depth perception
    • The objective of the project was to create two versions of this game and vary the haptic mechanism to see what effect that had on the players cognitive ability (memory).
    • Google "Haptic Pen" to see the type of device we used as our haptic controller.
