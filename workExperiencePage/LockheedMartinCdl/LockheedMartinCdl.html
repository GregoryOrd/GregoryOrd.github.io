Lockheed Martin Cdl Systems Internship (May 2020 to August 2021) (Remote due to COVID-19)

Lockheed Martin Cdl Systems

From the Lockheed Martin Website: "Lockheed Martin CDL Systems specializes in the development and licensing of vehicle control station software for unmanned systems."

During this internship I learned a lot about the software development process that CDL Systems uses to manage such a large team and ensure quality in their products. I practiced agile software development for the first time in this position, and learned a lot about things like test-driven development and continous integration. My skills with git also improved working here, and I gained a lot of technical knowledge about object oriented software and C++.



Video from Lockheed Martin Youtube Channel

How I Grew My Skills

Lockheed Martin CDL Systems has such a good group of people that even though my entire internship was working from home due to COVID-19, I made leaps and bounds learning from them. After working here, I know what it looks like to be a member of a strong professional team.
