Journey to Software Development in Calgary

Born and raised in Ontario, I was always an active and athletic child. My elementary school days revolved around games of hockey on the backyard rink or throwing the lacrosse ball around with friends at the park. Moving into highschool, my sporting endeavours shifted and running become my full time sport. This sport led me to Calgary where I joined the University of Calgary Dinos Mens Track and Cross-Country teams.

Upon arrival on the U of C campus in September of 2016, I began studying towards an undergraduate degree in engineering with the intent on applying to either the Electrical or Mechanical Engineering program. After taking the first year programming for engineering course, that plan quickly changed. I walked out of the course's final exam saying, "Wow, that exam was acutally fun!", and a passion for software was born.

I have since been studying in the Software Engineering program at the University of Calgary's Schulich School of Engineering, and have spent the last three years soaking up as much software and programming related knowledge as possible. Having reached a level where some of my work is interacting with the world, I am excited to continue developing my skills and advance what is still an early career producing reliable, high-quality software.

Developer, Runner, All Around Good Guy
